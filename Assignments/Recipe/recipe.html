Delicious Pizza

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup pizza sauce (tomato-based)
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup sliced onions
  • ½ cup sliced bell peppers (red, green, yellow)
  • ½ cup mushrooms (optional)
  • ¼ cup sweet corn
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • A pinch of oregano & chili flakes (for seasoning)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, salt, sugar, and yeast.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil, knead into soft dough.
  3. Cover and let it rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  5. Roll out the dough and spread pizza sauce evenly.
  6. Add mozzarella cheese, then top with onions, bell peppers, mushrooms, corn, and basil.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes until cheese melts and crust is golden.
  8. Sprinkle oregano & chili flakes before serving hot.
Enjoy your homemade Pizza!
